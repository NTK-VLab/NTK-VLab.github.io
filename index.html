About NTKVLab

Looking to develop an Internet of Things (IoT) product but lack expertise in firmware development and hardware design? Look no further! At NTKVLab We provide comprehensive solutions for IoT and embedded product development, including firmware development, hardware design, and research and development.

In addition to firmware development, We offer hardware design services. Whether you need a custom PCB design, sensor integration, or device prototyping, We've got you covered with the expertise to bring your IoT product to reality.

We specialize in STM32, ESP32, TI, and NXP MCU lineups, as well as Amazon FreeRTOS and AWS. I can deliver fully finished PCB designs in Altium and KiCad.

With our comprehensive IoT product development services, you can be confident that your product will be developed to the highest standards of quality, reliability, and security. Feel free to contact us!
